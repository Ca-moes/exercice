## ✅ Done: TS900 Power Tower

The #1 gap in the old plan was **no vertical pulling**. The TS900 fixes that and more:

- **Pull-ups / chin-ups / negatives** — the vertical pull that inverted rows can't replace
- **Dips** — a proper chest + triceps compound for Push day
- **Inverted rows on a real bar** — adjustable grip and width (the treadmill handles couldn't)
- **Dead hangs** — shoulder-flexion progression + spinal decompression for the postural routine
- **Balance support** for sissy squats and single-leg calf raises

At 42.5 kg it's planted and stable for dips and pull-ups, rated to 150 kg (room for a weighted vest/backpack later), and Decathlon's 2-year return + buy-back means it's easy to move on from if your setup changes.

## Decision Framework

Before buying, ask:

1. **Has the training trigger actually happened?** (don't buy ahead of need)
2. **Can I solve it with a progression instead?** (tempo, ROM, harder variation)
3. **Will this be used 3+ times per week?**
4. **Can I find it used?** (OLX, Facebook Marketplace)
